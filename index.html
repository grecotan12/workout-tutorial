What is Calisthenics?

Basic Calisthenics Movements

Calisthenics (American English) or callisthenics (British English) (/ˌkælɪsˈθɛnɪks/) is a form of strength training consisting of a variety of movements that exercise large muscle groups (gross motor movements), such as standing, grasping, pushing, etc. There are three basic movements you need to master which is push-up, pulls up and dips. You need to get to 10 pulls up in a row, 50 push ups in a row, and 20 dips in a row to progress to learn skills.
